Check out my spirit friends!

This is Lore. My loyal companion, Lore is a moon spirit who takes the form of a fox unicorn. He lost his eye fighting copper miners, and chooses not to restore it since he's edgy.

This is Sprig. A wood sprite, Sprig is a fun little lad that enjoys spooking campers by shaking their tents in the middle of the night and causing trees to move.

This is Terhaton. A wind spirit in the form of a winged snake, Terhaton glides all around Isle Royale. They spend their days setting new records for the speed in which they can cross the island.

This is Xyxyl. Xyxyl is an oak spirit that takes the form of a stump-shelled snail. She traverses the land, tending to her fellow oaks, and distributing acorns around as needed.
